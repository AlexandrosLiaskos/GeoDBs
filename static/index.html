Welcome to Historic Floods

Welcome to Historic Floods, an interactive map for exploring historic flood events.

Database Coverage: 1886–2022 | Geographic Scope: All of Greece

This interactive map is built upon the flood database published in the academic research paper by Evelpidou et al. (2023) in the journal Climate

How to cite:

Evelpidou, N., Cartalis, C., Karkani, A., Saitis, G., Philippopoulos, K., & Spyrou, E. (2023). A GIS-Based Assessment of Flood Hazard through Track Records over the 1886–2022 Period in Greece. Climate, 11, 226. https://doi.org/10.3390/cli11110226 Interactive map available at: https://HistoricFloods.org
